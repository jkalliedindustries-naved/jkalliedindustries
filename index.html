About Us

ISO 9001:2015 Certified Manufacturer

JK Allied Industries, founded by Naved Khan, is an ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer and stockist based in Bankra, Howrah, West Bengal (711403). We manufacture complete HT & LT transmission line hardware including Gang AB Switches, DO Fuse Sets, Single Break Switches, Brass & Aluminium Metal Castings, Hardware Fittings, Stay Set accessories and Overhead Line Materials.

Our firm has supplied complete accessories to various State Electricity Boards and DISCOMs across India as per their specifications. We are continuously developing new products for distribution line materials.
